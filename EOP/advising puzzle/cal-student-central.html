Cal Student Central (CSC) is a one-stop resource that includes the Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships, Office of the Registrar and Billing & Payment Services. CSC helps students understand their student budget, financial aid package (loans, grants/scholarships and work-study), appeals processes, etc. Additionally, CSC assists students with billing/payment inquiries, transcripts, enrollment verifications, residency and more.
